Boiled Water

Description

Boiling water

Ingredients

  • A pot
  • A stove
  • Water

Steps

  1. Place pot under tap
  2. Turn tap on.
  3. Fill pot with water.
  4. Turn tap off.
  5. Place pot on stove
  6. Turn stove on
  7. Wait until bubbly (don't touch)
  8. Turn stove off
  9. Enjoy your boiled water with a pinch of salt
