Ice Cubes

Description

Freezing cubes of ice

Ingredients

  • Water
  • An ice-cube tray
  • A very cold place
  • A place to nap

Steps

  1. Place the ice-cube tray under tap
  2. Turn tap on
  3. Fill ice-cube tray with water
  4. Turn tap off
  5. Place tray in a very cold place
  6. Take a long nap
  7. The tray is now filled with ice-cubes
